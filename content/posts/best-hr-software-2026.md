Finding the right HR software makes the difference between drowning in paperwork and running a scalable people operation. We tested five leading HR platforms to help small business owners choose the right fit for payroll, benefits, compliance, and employee management.

## The Short Answer

**BambooHR** for pure HR features and employee management. **Gusto** for integrated payroll and HR. **Rippling** for companies adding contractors and multiple locations. **Deel** for distributed and global teams. **Zoho People** for extreme value and budget-conscious companies.

- **Employee data management** - Directory, org chart, documents, profiles

- **Performance management** - Goals, reviews, feedback, succession planning
- **Onboarding workflow** - Document signing, equipment, access management

## 1. BambooHR — Best for Employee Management

**Best for:** Small to mid-size companies (10-500 employees), companies prioritizing employee experience

BambooHR is purpose-built for HR professionals who want deep employee management without payroll complexity. The platform includes employee directory, org charts, document management, and performance reviews. Time tracking integrates via third-party apps.

The interface is clean and modern. Onboarding workflows guide new hires through documents, training, and access setup. Employee self-service portal lets team members update their own information, request time off, and access pay stubs (when connected to payroll).

BambooHR doesn't handle payroll directly — you integrate with ADP, Gusto, or Rippling. This separation lets you choose the payroll provider that fits your needs.

- Integrates with popular payroll providers
- Good customer support

**Cons:**

- No benefits administration (separate integration)

## 3. Rippling — Best for Scaling Companies

**Best for:** Growing companies (20-500 employees), companies with contractors and multiple locations

Rippling is a modern HRIS that handles employees, contractors, devices, and access management. The platform automatically provisions new hire access — email, Slack, Github, etc. — in minutes rather than manually.

Contractor management is excellent: manage onboarding, contracts, and payments through one platform. Device management (company laptops, phones) includes device setup, provisioning, and deprovisioning when employees leave.

Payroll integrates with ADP or Rippling's own provider. Benefits administration is comprehensive. Compliance includes handbook templates and policy management tailored to company size.

**Rating:** 8.5/10
**Pricing:** $8/user/month (employees + contractors), minimum 10 seats
**Free trial:** 14 days

**Pros:**
- Modern, intuitive interface
- Device and access provisioning (huge time saver)
- Excellent contractor management
- Payroll flexibility (choose ADP or Rippling)
- Comprehensive benefits administration
- Strong integrations with IT tools
- Excellent for multi-location companies

## 4. Deel — Best for Global and Remote Teams

**Best for:** Companies with contractors worldwide, distributed teams, international expansion

Deel specializes in paying contractors and employees globally. The platform handles payroll in 150+ countries, manages contractor payments, and handles compliance with local labor laws.

If you have contractors in 5+ countries, Deel solves a massive headache. The platform handles currency conversion, tax compliance, and local statutory deductions automatically. Contractors receive payments in their local currency — no complicated wire transfers.

Employee features include contractor profiles, contracts with e-signature, invoicing, and payment processing. Time tracking is basic but adequate. Benefits administration is limited (mainly contractor-focused).

**Rating:** 8.2/10
**Pricing:** $49/contractor/month (annual billing) or $99 monthly, employees separate

- You have 10-500 employees
- You want HR features without complexity

**You should use Gusto if:**

**You should use Rippling if:**

**You should use Deel if:**
- You have contractors or employees worldwide
- You need compliance in multiple countries
- International payment is a key challenge

**You should use Zoho People if:**
- Budget is the primary constraint
- You use other Zoho products
