## 1. HubSpot — Best Free CRM

HubSpot's free CRM is genuinely the best starting point for most small businesses. Contact management, deal pipelines, email tracking, meeting scheduling — all free, with no time limit. The paid tiers become necessary as you scale marketing automation, but for pure CRM, free HubSpot is hard to beat.

**Free plan:** Yes — unlimited users, unlimited contacts
**Paid plans:** From $20/month (Starter)
**Best for:** Businesses wanting CRM + marketing tools in one place

**Pros:** Extremely polished UI, enormous ecosystem of integrations, free forever for core CRM
**Cons:** Paid tiers jump significantly in price; advanced features get expensive fast

[Try HubSpot Free →](https://hubspot.com) *(affiliate link)*

## 2. Pipedrive — Best for Sales Teams

Pipedrive is built entirely around the sales pipeline — it's visual, fast, and gets out of the way. If your primary use case is tracking deals through a sales funnel rather than marketing automation, Pipedrive is cleaner and more intuitive than HubSpot.

**Pricing:** From $14/month (Essential, billed annually)
**Free trial:** 14 days
**Best for:** Sales-focused SMBs with defined pipeline stages

[Try Pipedrive →](https://pipedrive.com) *(affiliate link)*

---

## 3. Zoho CRM — Best for Customisation on a Budget

Zoho CRM offers the most customisation at the lowest price point. Its free tier supports up to 3 users — enough for a small founding team — and the paid tiers are significantly cheaper than HubSpot's equivalent features. The trade-off is a steeper learning curve and a less polished interface.

**Free plan:** Yes — up to 3 users
**Paid plans:** From $20/month (Standard)
**Best for:** Budget-conscious teams who need flexibility

[Try Zoho CRM →](https://zoho.com/crm) *(affiliate link)*

---

## 4. ActiveCampaign — Best for Email + CRM Combined

ActiveCampaign blurs the line between email marketing platform and CRM. If your sales process is heavily email-driven — nurture sequences, automated follow-ups, lead scoring — ActiveCampaign does this better than any pure CRM. It's not the right choice if you just want a contacts database, but for email-led sales funnels it's excellent.

**Pricing:** From $29/month (Plus, billed annually)
**Free trial:** 14 days
**Best for:** Businesses with email-driven sales funnels

[Try ActiveCampaign →](https://activecampaign.com) *(affiliate link)*

## How to Choose

**Start with HubSpot free** if you're not sure what you need. You can always migrate later, and the free tier gives you a full picture of what CRM actually does for your business.

**Choose Pipedrive** if your team is sales-focused and you want the simplest, fastest pipeline tool.

**Choose Zoho** if you need customisation and HubSpot's paid tiers are out of budget.

**Choose ActiveCampaign** if email automation is the core of your sales process.
